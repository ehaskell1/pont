Rules

Joining a game

Gameplay is split into rooms, which are identified by a three-word code (assigned by the server).

To create a new room, enter your name and click "Create new room" (or press Enter). Your room's code will be in the top left; send it to your friends.

To join a room, enter your name and the code that your friend send you, then click "Join existing room" (or press Enter).

Taking your turn

On each turn, you may either place one man (black piece) or move the football (white piece).

A move of the football consists of one or more jumps, each jump over one or more adjacent men, horizontally, vertically, or diagonally, with the jumped men removed from the board (before any subsequent jump occurs).

A man can be placed anywhere on the light green region and its borders.

The football can be placed anywhere that men can in addition to the blue region.

Ending the game

The game is won by the player who can advance the football onto or over the opponent's goal line (blue region).

When the game ends, a new game is automatically started.
